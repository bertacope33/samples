LIVE BEAUTIFUL.



The newest addition to the Mercantile Place on Main community brings a higher level of sophistication to Downtown living.

Centered on a luxury spa theme focused on personal and environmental wellness The Continental offers a lifestyle unlike anything you'll find in the city. From morning workouts in the Technogym Fitness Training Center and Yoga Studio, to after work escapes in the hot tub, sauna and steam room, you'll have ample opportunity to energize your body and renew your inner-self.

The 11th floor terrace and lounge is an ideal backdrop for stimulating conversations, amazing feats of entertainment, rousing competitions, and peaceful moments of reflection viewing the cityscape spread out before you.
